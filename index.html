Our Story

Established in 1995 in the heart of Jaipur, Bhagwati Exports is a trusted name in fine jewelry, specializing in authentic Polki and diamond jewelry. Rooted in traditional craftsmanship and refined by contemporary design, we create timeless pieces that celebrate heritage, elegance, and purity.

We are proudly associated with Divine Solitaires, ensuring superior quality, certified diamonds, and uncompromising standards in every creation. Each piece is meticulously crafted using real gold and hand-set Polki, reflecting Jaipur’s rich legacy of jewelry artistry.

With decades of experience, Bhagwati Exports continues to serve discerning clients with integrity, precision, and a commitment to excellence—where tradition meets timeless luxury.

About Us






Founded in 1995 in Jaipur by Shikha Maheshwari, Bhagwati Exports was created with a vision to celebrate the timeless beauty of traditional Indian jewelry. With a passion for authentic Polki craftsmanship and fine diamonds, the brand blends Jaipur’s rich heritage with elegant, contemporary design. Today, Bhagwati Exports is known for its handcrafted Polki and diamond jewelry made in real gold, proudly associated with Divine Solitaires, and committed to quality, purity, and lasting elegance.
